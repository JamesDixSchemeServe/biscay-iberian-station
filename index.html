Firebase Setup Required

This app uses Firebase Realtime Database for live sync across all players' devices. You need a free Firebase project — setup takes about 5 minutes.

Step 1 — Create a Firebase project

Go to console.firebase.google.com, create a project (any name), then add a Realtime Database in test mode.

Step 2 — Get your config

In Project Settings → Your apps → Add web app. Copy the firebaseConfig object values.

Step 3 — Enter your config below

Config is stored in your browser only. Share the URL with players once connected — they enter the same config on first visit, or you can pre-fill it in the HTML.

Biscay & Iberian Station

The Biscay & Iberian Station Campaign is a custom made narrative campaign system for Black Seas, set during the naval war along the Atlantic coasts of France, Spain and Portugal between 1796 and 1808.

Take command of a squadron of Royal Navy, French or Spanish warships and fight a linked campaign where every action has lasting consequences. Ships suffer persistent damage, officers earn promotions or face disgrace, prizes are captured, repairs consume valuable resources, and victories influence the strategic situation across the station.

Featuring a wide variety of missions — from convoy escorts and commerce raiding to cutting-out expeditions, reconnaissance patrols and shore bombardments — the campaign is designed to tell the story behind each battle, creating an evolving record of your service through the Age of Sail.
